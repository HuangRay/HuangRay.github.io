Background

I am from Taiwan, a student at Tamkang University (TKU) of the master of the second year, and now, I am living in Tokyo, Japan, for one year of exchanging in the Eletro-Communication University (UEC). I came here to learn Japanese and do the research, and enjoy the Japan's life as well. The reason I came here is not only I like Japan but also that I know Japan is goot at robotics, thus, I want to learn from here.

I am researching about robotics. In Taiwan, My research is about mobile robot, and now in Japan is researching about snake-like robot. The mobile is a soccer robot which the purpose of the robot is to make it more intelligent so that the robot could automatically find the target and choose a strategy to attack the opposite goal. I am in charge of the strategy and the controller of the robot. Our team join to the Federation of International Robot Soccer Association (FIRA) which is a competition of the robot to make our robot more competitive. In Japan, I am going to research about snake-like robot, and it a new field for me. I know it would be difficult but I still want to try it.

Except the research and studying, sometimes I went around the Tokyo in the weekend by train. My purpose of exchanging is not only for researching all the day though it is still an important part but I wnat to know Japan and improve Japanese as well.

Research

As I talk before, I research about the robots, including mobile robot and snake-like robot, and I will briefly introduce both robots.

Mobile robot

As the name of mobile robot, it must should be able to move. In TKU, I research the robots which the purpose is to play the soccer games are with the three wheels, and it is not controlled by human, in others words, the robot shoud move automatically which is based on the environment or the situations, so the robots should judge the environment or the situations and then make the corresponding behaviors. The most special place of the robot is that A robot has three wheels, called omnidirectional wheel, and a omnidirectional vision system. Because the robot is for the soccer games, the omnidirectional wheels could make the robot moves fast and turns smoothly and the omnidirectional vision system could make the robot watches 360 degrees around the robot, namely, the robot can know all of the situations around itself.
